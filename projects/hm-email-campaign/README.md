# Email Marketing Campaign Design for H&M

## Project Overview

Designed a promotional email marketing campaign showcasing H&M's latest seasonal fashion collection. This project focused on creating visually engaging email designs that highlight versatile wardrobe essentials while driving traffic to the online store.

## Project Details

**Client**: H&M  
**Project Type**: Email Marketing Campaign  
**Status**: Completed  
**Year**: 2026

## Objectives

- Showcase H&M's latest seasonal fashion collection
- Appeal to fashion-conscious individuals
- Drive traffic to the online store
- Increase engagement and conversions

## Design Approach

### Visual Strategy
- **Style**: Clean, modern layout with vibrant, eye-catching visuals
- **Focus**: Stylish, high-quality clothing with new arrivals and trending pieces
- **User Experience**: Easy navigation for a seamless, intuitive experience

### Key Features
- ✨ High-quality product photography and mockups
- 🎯 Strategic calls-to-action: "Shop Now" and "Explore New Arrivals"
- 📱 Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices
- 🎨 Brand-consistent color palette and typography
- ➡️ Clear visual hierarchy guiding users to conversion points

## Tools & Technologies Used

- **Adobe Photoshop** - Image editing and graphic design
- **Figma** - Design layout and prototyping

## Results & Impact

- Highlighted versatile wardrobe essentials to appeal to target audience
- Created compelling visual hierarchy to guide user attention
- Designed clear CTAs to maximize click-through rates and online store traffic
- Delivered a professional email template ready for campaign deployment
